❌ Meta Quest 3: Cons

✔️ Seamless Apple ecosystem integration

❌ Apple Vision Pro: Cons

❌ Extremely expensive ($3,499)
❌ Heavy
❌ Limited game library
❌ Short battery life

⭐ Final Verdict

For gamers and entertainment: Meta Quest 3 is the best choice with a reasonable price and endless games.
For professionals and Apple users: Apple Vision Pro is the future of spatial computing, but at a premium price.
